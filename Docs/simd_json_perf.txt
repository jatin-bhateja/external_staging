Utf8Validator with slice API is performant on AVX512 targets even without ALIGNR based acceleration
But, on AVX2/E-core platforms only ALIGNR based slice will be performant.

On AMD targets, where clint and servers both support AVX512 ISA, slice based Utf8Validator will always
be performant even with stock JDK-24.0.2.

While on Intel targets we need to wait till integration of JDK-8303762 and bumping of gradle to jdk-25+,
JDK-8290322 improved the byte vector rearrange on AVX2/E-core targets but its performance does not
match up top VPERMB.
